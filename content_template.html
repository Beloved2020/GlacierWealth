Glacier Investment Counsel, Inc., takes an against-the-grain approach to investment and financial management, conducting our business with full and complete transparency. Because we earn our compensation via agreed-upon flat fees instead of on a transaction basis, we are free to make decisions that are consistent with your financial objectives—not ours or those of our investment partners.

 

Our clientele is comprised of a limited number of individuals who understand the difference between investing and speculating, and who possess the emotional discipline necessary to continue to pursue their long-term goals through market fluctuations. They assume a mutual and shared responsibility for their financial future, which is achieved through a relationship founded on frank discussions and long-term focus.

 

As well, we work with corporate retirement plan sponsors in a fiduciary partner capacity.

 

Glacier Investment Counsel, Inc., brings more than a decade of investment and financial management experience. We employ our extensive financial knowledge to craft prudent and appropriate strategies to meet our client’s objectives.
