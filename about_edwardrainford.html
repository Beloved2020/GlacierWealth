GlacierWealth Principal Edward Rainford has a long history of providing hands-on counsel to companies and wealthy individuals and families.

 

Based in Boise, Mr. Rainford graduated from Willamette University in Salem, Ore., with a bachelor’s degree in Business Economics and a minor in Religious Studies. He served as a financial advisor in the conventional Wall Street model, which led to his determination that fee-only financial and investment management was the fairest way to serve his clients. In 2001, he founded a registered investment advisory firm that is now GlacierWealth, Inc., to focus his passion and energy solely on his clients' financial well-being.

 

Edward often says, “You can’t tell people you’re honest—you have to show them.” And that’s why he strives to bring clarity, passion, and, of course, increased wealth to each and every one of his clients. GlacierWealth requires all third-party partners to share his same standards of transparency, objectivity, and responsiveness.
